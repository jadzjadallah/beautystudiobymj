ABOUT MJ


MJ is a qualified beauty therapist who started her passion in 2015 and she's the founder of BEAUTYSTUDIO by MJ.

After developing an interest in beauty at a young age, MJ had manu professional courses to turn her passion into applied art.

At the salon, she offers a personal yet professional service in a dedicated beauty environment with a huge variety of treatments available. MJ always takes great pride in her work and makes sure she is constantly updating her skills to ensure her knowledge is up-to-date with the latest trends and techniques available.

"The number one goal is for you to enjoy the experience and to leave feeling completely refreshed and revitalised."
